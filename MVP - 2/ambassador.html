Campus Ambassador Program

We are looking for college students to help up spread the word about Harpett

There are a number of benefits to becoming a Campus Ambassador, including helping your fellow students acheive their potential, while simultaneously developing many of the skills asked for by employers. There are a number of features available on Harpett that extend across the curriculum with many students being unaware of the value of offerings such as ours.

Another great benefit of our program is that we are assembling a Student Advisory Board composed of our Campus Ambassadors. Serving on this board will allow your voice to be heard as to what resources are valuable to students. On this board you would work closely with Harpett's leaders in pushing the technological resource vision forward.
